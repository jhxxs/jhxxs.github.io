Youtube Audio to Apple Music Album

I didn't own the game Duck Detective yet, but I really enjoy its OST.

Luckily, its youtube channel uploaded the OST in separate chapters.

With the help of ffmpeg, I made an album for personal use only. 😹 Sorry, @HappyBroccoliGames.

1. download audio in split chapters​

yt-dlp --extract-audio --audio-format mp3 --split-chapters <YouTube URL>

2. select all and open with Apple Music​

3. upload to library​

4. edit every song's description​

notice the track info, the first number is the actual order
